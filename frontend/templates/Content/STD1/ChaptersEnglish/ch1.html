Chapter One

Once upon a time, in a lush green forest, there lived a curious little rabbit named Ruby. Ruby loved to explore the woods and make new friends. One sunny morning, Ruby decided to visit the wise old owl, who lived on the other side of the forest. As she hopped along the path, she saw colorful flowers, tall trees, and heard the sweet songs of the birds.
